About Northumbria University

Northumbria University is a research-intensive institution committed to unlocking potential for all— transforming lives regionally, nationally, and globally. Named UK University of the Year 2022 (Times Higher Education) and Modern University of the Year 2025 (The Times & The Sunday Times), Northumbria is located in Newcastle upon Tyne with an additional London campus.

Originating from Rutherford College in 1880, Northumbria is ranked among the UK’s top 25 for research power and graduate employability. The university collaborates with world-leading employers such as Nike, IBM, and the NHS, and is recognised as one of the UK’s top institutions for graduate start-ups.

With students from over 137 countries and extensive global partnerships, Northumbria continues to drive innovation, entrepreneurship, and world-changing academic excellence.
